Our Story

Providence Plumbing, Providence Values

Kwik Plumbing was founded in 2005 by Providence native Mike Kwiatkowski — a third-generation plumber who learned the trade from his father and grandfather, both of whom spent their careers serving Providence homes and businesses. The name "Kwik" is a nod to the family surname, a promise to customers, and a statement of values: responsive, reliable, and always available when you need us most.

What started as a one-man operation out of a pickup truck has grown into a full-service Providence plumbing company with a fleet of fully-stocked service vans, a team of licensed and trained technicians, and a reputation built entirely on word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied Providence customers. We've never spent a dollar on a national franchise fee — every dollar we invest goes into better equipment, better training, and better service for our Providence community.

Over 20 years and more than 4,500 completed jobs later, we're still here — still local, still answering the phone personally, and still treating every Providence homeowner and business owner the way we'd want our own family treated.

Our Team

Every plumber on the Kwik Plumbing team is Rhode Island state-licensed and has completed a formal apprenticeship through the PHCC (Plumbing-Heating-Cooling Contractors Association) program. We don't hire unlicensed laborers or subcontract work to strangers — every technician who enters your Providence home is a vetted Kwik employee who has passed background checks and ongoing technical training.

Our team collectively brings decades of experience with Providence's diverse housing stock — from the 19th-century brick Victorians of College Hill to the post-war triple-deckers of Silver Lake, from downtown commercial buildings to the newer residential developments along the Providence waterfront.

Our Commitment to Providence

We take our role as a Providence business seriously. We hire locally, purchase materials from Rhode Island suppliers when possible, and give back to our community through sponsorships of Providence youth sports leagues and annual participation in Habitat for Humanity builds in the greater Providence area. When you hire Kwik Plumbing, you're keeping money in Providence — not sending it to a corporate headquarters somewhere else.
